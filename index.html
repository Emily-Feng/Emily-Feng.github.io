About Me

Hi! I am a multimedia fashion designer and illustrator based in New York City. Currently a sophomore studying in Parsons School of Design, I am pursuing a BFA degree in Fashion Design and a minor in Creative Entrepreneurship. My passion for visual arts was inspired by my grandmother’s practice of Chinese traditional painting, and has drawn me to all sorts of mediums, including oil paint, watercolor, and digital. Since moving to New York City, I started to experiment more in 3D arts, which led to my discovering an enthusiasm for fashion. My works focus on self-expression and exploring my own cultural identity. I continue to use fashion design and other sorts of of mediums to communicate ideas.
